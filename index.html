What we do

Big ideas about AI, made for young minds.

Founded in 2024, TheLearnAI Program brings artificial intelligence out of the headlines and into the hands of kids. Through games, live demos, and guided projects, students discover what AI really is, how it makes decisions, and where it gets things wrong.

Every session is led in person by trained teachers who make the concepts click — from how a chatbot "guesses" to why a robot might insist that every apple is red. Kids leave able to think about AI with curiosity and confidence, not confusion.

Inside a lesson

Curiosity in, confidence out.

Our lessons are built to be felt, not just heard. Here's what a student takes away.

🤖

How AI works

Students see how machines learn from examples and make predictions — using games and demos they can touch and try themselves.

🍎

Why AI gets it wrong

Through activities like the robot that thinks every apple is red, kids learn about bias and bad data — and why AI needs careful humans.

🚀

Using AI wisely

We build the habits of a responsible AI user: asking good questions, checking answers, and staying curious about how tools really work.
